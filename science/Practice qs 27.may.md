[[Atoms]] are made up of [[subatomic particles]]. There is a dense nucleus made up of [[neutron]]s and [[proton]]s, with neutrons having a neutral / no charge, and protons having a positive charge. There are electrons orbiting the nucleus at fixed levels, electrons have a negative charge and are smaller than protons and neutrons.

[[Ions]] are atoms which have lost or gained electrons. If they have lost electrons they are a cation, and if they have gained electrons they are anions.
